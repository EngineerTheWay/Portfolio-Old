About Me

I’m a U.S. Air Force veteran transitioning into Tech, with a background in cyber operations, classroom instruction, and cross-functional leadership. I’m passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and help organizations work smarter.

With nearly a decade in the military, I’ve built a foundation in strategic thinking, problem-solving, and team leadership. I’ve led interdisciplinary teams, implemented process improvements, and worked across technical and non-technical environments to drive impact.

As I pivot into the private sector, I’m focused on growing as a both technical expert and business leader. I'm currently pursuing a Master's in IT, developing expertise in project management, cybersecurity, cloud, and enterprise IT systems.

Professional Objectives

As I prepare to separate from active duty in November 2026, I’m seeking future opportunities within cybersecurity, cloud, and IT that will allow me to further develop my technical skillset. I’ll be SkillBridge-eligible starting June 2026 and open to roles in the San Francisco Bay Area, Seattle, or Denver.

Looking ahead, I plan to use the Post-9/11 GI Bill to pursue a full-time MBA in Fall 2027 to deepen my leadership and business strategy skills. My long-term goal is to bridge technology and business by helping organizations build secure systems, lead high-performing teams, and drive innovation at scale.
